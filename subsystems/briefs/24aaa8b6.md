TITLE: Swarm Self-Healer
DESCRIPTION: Subscribes to monitoring signals for blocked agents and gate failures, queries the Memories knowledge graph for matching past failures and their resolutions, and delivers structured recovery briefings to the affected agent or team lead for autonomous error recovery without human intervention.
SUBSYSTEM: Ichor.Swarm.SelfHealer
SIGNAL_INTERFACE: Subscribes to :monitoring category (agent_blocked, gate_failed); emits swarm_recovery_suggested, swarm_healing_attempted, swarm_pattern_unknown
TOPIC: swarm:self_healer
VERSION: 0.1.0
FEATURES: failure pattern matching, graph-backed resolution retrieval, recovery briefing delivery, failure episode ingestion, ETS-based dedup window
USE_CASES: agent stuck on compile error, repeated gate failure, blocked team lead, cascade failure detection, failure knowledge accumulation across sessions
ARCHITECTURE: Supervised GenServer that subscribes to the :monitoring signal category. On agent_blocked or gate_failed signal: (1) query GraphMemory for similar past failures using session_id and error context, (2) compose structured recovery briefing from top-3 graph results, (3) send_message to blocked agent or its team lead via Fleet.Agent, (4) ingest the new failure + attempted resolution as an episode to project:ichor:fleet space. ETS table tracks seen {session_id, error_hash} pairs with 5-minute TTL to prevent duplicate briefings on repeated signals.
DEPENDENCIES: Ichor.Signals, Ichor.AgentTools.GraphMemory, Ichor.Archon.MemoriesClient, Ichor.Fleet.Agent, Ichor.Gateway.Router, Ichor.Fleet.ThreadRegistry
SIGNALS_EMITTED: swarm_recovery_suggested, swarm_healing_attempted, swarm_pattern_unknown
SIGNALS_SUBSCRIBED: agent_blocked, gate_failed
